Debriefing


This experiment was concerned with the use of diagrams in abstract reasoning. You were randomly assigned to one of two conditions: either your diagrams were "schematic" ***, or they were "mechanistic" such that the Euler solution could be mentally simulated as a physical mechanism. The experiment investigated whether mechanistic diagrams, by virtue of their concreteness, improve performance in syllogistic reasoning.
