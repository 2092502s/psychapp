Welcome to participate in an experiment about reasoning

Here's some additional information:

  • You need to be above 18 and English-speaking in order to participate.

  • The experiment is estimated to take between 30-40 minutes.

  • The experiment concerns reasoning processes, but will in no way test your intelligence.

  • After a short tutorial on diagrams, you will be asked to complete a set of 19 small reasoning problems.

  • Please stay focused and do the experiment in a quiet place, without other tabs or programs open.

  • Participation is completely voluntary. You are free to withdraw from the experiment at any time, but please don't take any break during the duration of it.

  • Your participation is anonymous and completely confidential: personal data will be safely stored separately from experimental data.

  • Please do not participate more than once. If your internet connection fails or the experiment is interrupted, your data must be rejected, so please do not attempt it again.

  • The results will be used in a dissertation project at the University of Glasgow.

  • If you have questions or if you discover bugs in the website, please contact me at 2092502s@student.gla.ac.uk
